Troubleshooting

  • Controls feel unresponsive: Ensure you are not touching multiple UI regions accidentally; on-device gestures may override controls.
  • Game feels slow or laggy: Close background apps or restart your device.
  • Weapon cooldown seems stuck: Exit to the main menu and re-enter the game.
  • Maze elements not rendering: Restart the app; if persistent, reinstall Maze Rampage.
  • Sound not working: Check device volume and toggle sound settings inside the game.
