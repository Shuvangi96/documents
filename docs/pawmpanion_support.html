Subscriptions (RevenueCat)

Pawmpanion offers optional subscription tiers: Premium and Elite. Purchases are handled securely via RevenueCat and the App Store.

  • Purchase: Tap Upgrade in the app to view current offerings and complete checkout via Apple's payment sheet.
  • Restore purchases: If you switch devices, open the app and use the "Restore Purchases" button in Profile → Subscriptions. This calls the platform restore flow.
  • Manage or Cancel: Manage or cancel subscriptions from your Apple ID account settings.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashes / freezes: Force-quit the app and reopen. If the problem persists, reinstall the app. Please attach crash logs when emailing support.
  • Audio not playing: Check device volume and silent switch. Ensure app audio permission is enabled and try restarting the device.
  • Network errors: Pawmpanion uses a cloud LLM for replies. If you see network errors, check connectivity; in offline mode the app will still show cached replies where available.
  • Missing paid features after purchase: Try Restore Purchases first. If it still fails, contact support with the purchase receipt from Apple.
  • How do I export or backup chats? Chats and memories are currently local. We plan to add safe export tools in a future update; contact support if you need assistance.

FAQ

Is Pawmpanion safe for children?
Pawmpanion is not designed specifically for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
Does Pawmpanion store my conversations on a server?
No — chats, bond data, and Soul memories are stored locally on your device by default. Subscription status may be validated via RevenueCat.
How private is Soul / Core Memories data?
The Soul is a lightweight summary stored locally to personalize conversations. It is not shared externally except as part of responses to the LLM when you opt into cloud features; check the privacy policy for details.
I changed devices — how do I move my data?
There is no automatic cross-device sync currently. We recommend using device backups (iCloud) to transfer app data. Contact support for help with migration options.
